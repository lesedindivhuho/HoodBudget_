## About HoodBudget

HoodBudget is a modern Android budgeting application built to help users manage their money in a simple, clean, and practical way. The app focuses on making everyday expense tracking feel easy while still giving users clear visibility into their spending habits.

With HoodBudget, users can create local accounts, organise their spending with categories, log detailed expenses, attach photos to entries, set monthly spending goals, and review category totals over selected periods. Everything is stored locally on the device using Room, making the app lightweight and offline-friendly.

---

## Vision

HoodBudget aims to become a smart personal finance companion that feels approachable, stylish, and useful for real daily life. The goal is not just to record spending, but to help users build stronger financial habits through a smooth experience and clearer awareness of where their money goes.

In the future, HoodBudget can grow into a richer budgeting platform with deeper analytics, stronger planning tools, reminders, and more advanced financial insights.

---

## Current Features

* Local account creation and login
* Category creation and management
* Expense tracking with amount, date, time, description, and category
* Optional photo attachment for expense entries
* Monthly minimum and maximum spending goals
* Expense history filtered by a user-selected date range
* Category spending totals for a selected period
* Offline local storage with Room Database
